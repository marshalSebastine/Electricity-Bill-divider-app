Tiltle: Flat Mate's Bill
Description:
       Design an app which has the number of days each flat mates stayed in 
       the apartment in a given period and the bill amount in that period.
       The app calculates the portion of bill each flat mates has to pay on the basis 
       of number of day each flatmate spent in the apartment.App finally prints  pdf 
       report showing the flat mate's name and the amount each has to pay
Objects:
     flatmate:
         name
         in_days
         amount_to_pay(bill)

    Bill:
       amount
       period
    PdfReport:
       filename
       generate(flatmate,bill)
